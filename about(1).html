About Roma Khan

Roma Khan is a certified celebrity makeup and hairstylist based in Hyderabad. Known for her magical bridal transformations and trendy party looks, she has worked with Manara and styled renowned models like Leena, Amiksha, and Iba Khan. With a passion for glam and precision, Roma brings out the star in every bride and model she touches.
