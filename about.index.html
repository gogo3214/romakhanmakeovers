About Roma Khan

Roma Khan is a certified celebrity makeup and hairstyle artist based in Hyderabad, known for her refined artistry, luxurious techniques, and signature glam finishes.

With a passion for enhancing natural beauty and a strong eye for detail, Roma has become a trusted name in the bridal and fashion industries.

She has worked with renowned personalities including actress Manara Chopra, and styled popular models like Leena, Amiksha, and Iba Khan.

Whether it’s a wedding, fashion campaign, or red carpet event, Roma’s work reflects professionalism, creativity, and a deep passion for beauty.
